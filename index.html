Our Flooring & Renovation Services

From beautiful new floors to complete home renovations, we bring expertise and craftsmanship to every project.

LVP Flooring

Luxury Vinyl Plank that combines stunning aesthetics with unmatched durability. Waterproof and perfect for any room.

Carpet

Premium carpet installation with a wide selection of styles, textures, and colors. Professional fitting for a flawless finish.

Wood Flooring

Timeless hardwood floors that add warmth and value to your home. Expert installation, refinishing, and restoration.

Tile Flooring

Beautiful ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone tile for kitchens, bathrooms, and entryways. Precision installation.

Garage & Basement

About KY Flooring

A Family Legacy of Craftsmanship
