Our Flooring & Renovation Services

LVP Flooring

Luxury Vinyl Plank flooring that combines stunning aesthetics with unmatched durability. Waterproof and perfect for any room in your home.

Carpet

Premium carpet installation with a wide selection of styles, textures, and colors. Professional fitting that ensures a flawless finish.

Hardwood Flooring

Timeless hardwood floors that add warmth and value to your home. Expert installation, refinishing, and restoration available.

Tile Flooring

Beautiful ceramic, porcelain, and natural stone tile for kitchens, bathrooms, and entryways. Precision installation guaranteed.

Garage & Basement

Cabinets & Vanities

Moldings & Hardware

Finishing touches that make a difference. Crown molding, baseboards, door hardware, and trim work for a polished look.

About KY Flooring

A Family Legacy of Craftsmanship

Founded by Wayne Lacey in 1996, KY Flooring has grown from a small local operation into Central Kentucky's most trusted name in flooring and home renovation. For over 28 years, we've built our reputation one floor at a time.

We're not just contractors. We're your neighbors. Every project gets the same personal attention and quality craftsmanship we'd put into our own homes. That's the KY Flooring difference.
